Stories that already know your family.

Add each child once, choose a world and a length, then press play. TaleBird writes, paints a cover, and narrates a new story made for that moment.

Your child is the hero

Names, ages, favorite worlds, and little details become part of the adventure instead of being pasted on top.

Audio first

Warm narration means bedtime can stay eyes-closed, and car rides can stay hands-free over Bluetooth.

Daytime discoveries

When the questions start before bedtime, TaleBird can answer curious "why" and "how" moments in a gentle two-voice format.

Built for the back seat, not just the pillow.

Queue one story per child, autoplay through the list, and control playback from the lock screen or the car's own Bluetooth controls. It is made for real family motion: errands, road trips, and the long quiet five minutes before sleep.
